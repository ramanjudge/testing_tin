Interim & Contract Staffing

White-Collar Professionals, Deployed When You Need Them

Senior functional professionals on TiNAAK's payroll — available for fixed-term cover, open position gap-filling, or trial-to-hire evaluation. No permanent headcount commitment. Full statutory compliance. Deployment within days, not weeks.

Interim Management Cover
A senior role is vacant and the permanent search is taking time. Deploy a proven professional immediately — they run the function while you recruit properly.
Maternity & Medical Leave Cover
Fixed-duration, defined-scope replacement for employees on extended leave. Structured handover in, structured handover out — no disruption to the team or the client.
Trial-to-Hire Placement
Evaluate a candidate in the role before committing to permanent employment. The professional works on TiNAAK's payroll for 60–90 days. Convert directly if the fit is confirmed — or we replace if it is not.
Project-Based Deployment
Fit-out project managers, FM mobilisation leads, HR implementation specialists — deployed for the project duration and cleanly off-boarded at completion.
Specialist Gap Cover
A specific skill set is needed for a defined period — a compliance audit, a system implementation, a site launch. We provide the professional; you focus on the outcome.
Bench & Rapid Deployment
We maintain a pre-verified pool of professionals between roles across FM, HR, Finance, Admin and Operations — reducing deployment time to 48–72 hours for most profiles.
