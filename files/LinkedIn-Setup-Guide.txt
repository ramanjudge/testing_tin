ABOUT  (paste exactly as written — 1,847 characters)

Most growing organisations manage 6–10 separate service vendors for functions that should work
as one — housekeeping, maintenance, security, hiring, staffing, compliance. The coordination
overhead costs more than most organisations track. TiNAAK is built to close that gap.

TiNAAK Integrated Services LLP is a Gurugram-based professional services firm delivering five
integrated service lines:

· Managed Facility Operations — housekeeping, technical maintenance, security, transport and
  consumables managed end-to-end under a single SLA

· Professional Recruitment — mid to senior hiring across FM, HR, Finance, Admin and Operations,
  with depth built across 26 years of enterprise hiring practice

· Interim & Contract Staffing — white-collar professionals on TiNAAK's payroll, deployed for
  cover, project duration or trial-to-hire, available within days

· Corporate Fit-Outs & Projects — office fit-outs, renovations and multi-site rollouts delivered
  with documented governance and zero disruption to operations

· Advisory, Training & Products — independent FM and HR advisory, compliance reviews,
  ISO readiness consulting and practitioner training, backed by 8 published professional works

TiNAAK is founded by Raman Deep Singh Judge — 26 years of P&L ownership across ITES, Healthcare,
Manufacturing, FMCG and Food Processing; 2.5M+ sq ft of managed space across 30 cities. The firm's
credibility is practitioner-built, not theoretical.

Compliance is not an add-on at TiNAAK — it is the baseline. Every engagement is structured around
India's Labour Codes, ESIC, PF and contract labour obligations, with documentation maintained
audit-ready from day one.

Serving enterprise organisations in Gurugram, Delhi NCR and across India.

POST 2 — Compliance angle (publish day 3)

India's four Labour Codes create a specific, enforceable liability that most
organisations discover at audit — not before.

The principal employer is jointly responsible for the statutory failures of their
contractors. PF, ESIC, minimum wages, contract labour obligations — these apply
whether or not you engaged the workers directly.

At TiNAAK, compliance is the baseline. Every engagement comes with:
· Monthly PF/ESIC compliance certificates
· Properly structured appointment documentation
· Audit-ready records from day one
· POSH and workplace dignity standards across all deployments

If you would like a compliance review of your current FM or workforce arrangements,
speak with us.
