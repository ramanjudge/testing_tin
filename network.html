Our Standards

What We Expect
from Every Partner

The TiNAAK network is curated, not open. Every partner is held to the same baseline standards that protect our clients and our reputation.

Joining the TiNAAK network is not transactional. It is a professional relationship governed by a written agreement. Partners who consistently deliver above expectations are given priority on new engagements and elevated within the network tier structure. Those who do not meet standards are exited.

  • 01
    Verified Identity & Registration
    All partners must provide valid identity, GST/PAN and business registration where applicable. Individual specialists provide professional credentials and references.
  • 02
    Documented Capability
    Portfolio, certifications and work samples are reviewed before acceptance. Creative and technical disciplines require sample submissions.
  • 03
    Code of Conduct Agreement
    Every partner signs TiNAAK's code of conduct — covering professional behaviour, confidentiality, client relationship conduct and escalation protocols.
  • 04
    SLA Commitment
    Suppliers and specialists agree to specific service level commitments covering quality, timeliness and communication standards.
  • 05
    Performance Rating
    Every engagement is rated. Partners with consistently high ratings receive priority matching, preferred tier status and enhanced commission rates over time.
