Our Story

Why TiNAAK Exists

The problem TiNAAK is built to solve is not a shortage of vendors. India has an abundance of vendors. The problem is a shortage of integration — of a single partner who takes genuine ownership of multiple operational functions and delivers them to a consistent, documented, accountable standard.

Every time an organisation has to manage a separate contract for housekeeping, a separate agency for recruitment, a separate firm for its fit-out, a separate provider for payroll — it absorbs coordination cost, compliance risk and quality inconsistency that it cannot afford to carry.

TiNAAK was founded to change that for organisations across Gurugram, Delhi NCR and eventually pan-India. One partner. Clear accountability. Structured delivery.

Five Operating Values

The principles that govern every engagement, every hire and every partnership TiNAAK enters into.

Accountability over Activity

Growth for Everyone Involved

Leadership

A Founder Who Has

TiNAAK is founded by a practitioner with over 26 years of hands-on enterprise leadership across facility management, administration, HR, workforce operations and corporate projects. That experience spans ITES, manufacturing, FMCG and healthcare — across organisations ranging from growing startups to large multinational operations.

The credibility behind TiNAAK is not theoretical.
