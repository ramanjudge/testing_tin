Common Questions
What is your minimum engagement size?
We work with organisations of all sizes — from a 20-person office needing housekeeping services to a multi-site operation requiring integrated FM and HR. There is no minimum headcount or contract value to engage TiNAAK.
Which geographies do you currently serve?
Our primary geography is Gurugram and Delhi NCR. We are expanding across India through our Strategic Alliance Network — for requirements outside NCR, please send us your enquiry and we will advise on feasibility.
Can we engage you for just one service?
Yes, absolutely. Clients typically start with one service and expand the engagement as they experience the value of working with a structured partner. There is no obligation to take multiple services.
How quickly can you mobilise?
For managed services and staffing, we can typically mobilise within 2–4 weeks of contract signing depending on the scope. Fit-out projects begin with a discovery and scoping phase that takes 1–2 weeks before work commences.
Do you provide contractual documentation?
Yes. Every TiNAAK engagement is governed by a written service agreement covering scope, SLAs, payment terms, escalation protocols and termination clauses. We operate correctly — documentation is not optional.
